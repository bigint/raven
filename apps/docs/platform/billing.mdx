## Managing Your Subscription

Navigate to **Billing** in the dashboard to:

- View your current plan and usage
- Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- Switch between monthly and yearly billing
- Update payment methods
- View invoice history

## Payment

Billing is handled securely through LemonSqueezy. We accept all major credit cards.

### Yearly Billing

Save 20% by switching to yearly billing. The discount is applied immediately and your billing cycle resets.

## Enterprise

Enterprise plans include:

- **Unlimited** everything
- **Dedicated support** with SLA
- **Custom contracts** and invoicing
- **On-premise deployment** option
- **SSO/OIDC** with JIT provisioning
- **365-day** analytics retention
