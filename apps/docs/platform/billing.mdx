## Managing Your Subscription

Navigate to **Billing** in the dashboard to:

- View your current plan and usage
- Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- Switch between monthly and yearly billing
- Update payment methods
- View invoice history

### Upgrading

When you upgrade:

- The new plan takes effect immediately
- You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing period
- Higher resource limits apply instantly
- Previously unavailable features unlock immediately

### Downgrading

When you downgrade:

- The change takes effect at the end of the current billing period
- If you exceed the new plan's limits, you will need to reduce resources before the downgrade completes
- Features exclusive to higher plans become unavailable when the downgrade activates

<Warning>
  Before downgrading, check that your current usage does not exceed the target plan's limits. For example, if you have 15 virtual keys and downgrade from Team to Pro (max 20 keys), you are fine. But if you have 25 keys, you will need to delete some first.
</Warning>
