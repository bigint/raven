Teams let you group organization members for access control, budgeting, and analytics.

## Creating a Team

Navigate to **Team** in the dashboard and click **Create Team**.

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Team display name |
| **Members** | Users to add to the team |

## Team Features

### Budget Allocation

Assign budgets at the team level to control spending per group:

```
Organization: $1000/month
  ├── Engineering Team: $500/month
  ├── Marketing Team: $300/month
  └── Research Team: $200/month
```

### Analytics

View usage analytics filtered by team to understand spending and usage patterns per group.

### Access Control

Team members inherit the team's permissions and budget constraints.

## Team Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to team settings and members |
| **Admin** | Manage team members and view analytics |
| **Member** | Use team resources |
