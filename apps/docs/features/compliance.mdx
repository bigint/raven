Raven's compliance module helps you map your AI usage policies to industry compliance frameworks, track coverage, and generate reports for audits.

## Compliance Frameworks

Define or import compliance frameworks that your organization needs to adhere to:

- **GDPR** — General Data Protection Regulation
- **HIPAA** — Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
- **SOC 2** — Service Organization Control 2
- **ISO 27001** — Information Security Management
- **Custom frameworks** — Define your own organizational standards

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define a framework">
    Create or import a compliance framework with its requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map policies">
    Link your Raven policies to specific framework requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track coverage">
    See which requirements are covered by active policies and which have gaps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate reports">
    Export compliance reports for auditors showing policy coverage and evaluation history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Policy Evaluations

Track how your policies are performing over time:

- **Pass rate** — Percentage of requests that comply with all policies
- **Violation trends** — Track policy violations over time
- **Framework coverage** — Percentage of framework requirements covered by policies
