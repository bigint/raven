---
title: "Evaluations"
description: "Systematically evaluate model outputs with scoring and pass/fail criteria."
---

Evaluations let you measure the quality of LLM outputs against defined criteria, either automatically or through manual review.

## Evaluation Types

### Automated Evaluation

Use an LLM to judge the quality of another LLM's output:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Criteria** | What to evaluate (accuracy, helpfulness, safety) |
| **Scoring** | Numeric score (1-5) or pass/fail |
| **Sample rate** | Percentage of requests to evaluate |

### Manual Evaluation

Human reviewers assess outputs:

- **Pass/Fail** — Binary quality assessment
- **Scored** — Numeric rating with comments
- **Comparative** — Side-by-side comparison of variants

## Evaluation Results

Results are aggregated and displayed:

- **Pass rate** — Percentage of outputs that meet criteria
- **Average score** — Mean quality score
- **Score distribution** — Breakdown of scores
- **Trend analysis** — Quality changes over time

## Integration with Experiments

Evaluations can be attached to experiments to provide quality metrics for each variant, enabling data-driven model selection.
